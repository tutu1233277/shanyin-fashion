This project is a visual production for local R&B singer songwriter Kwanchen's debut album Alone, released by the American music company 88rising.

I worked as the fashion stylist and hair and makeup artist, developing visual identities that translated the album's emotional atmosphere into styling, colour, texture, and character design.

In the music video Escape, Kwanchen is imagined as a mysterious "blue man", wearing garments treated with raindrop and tear like elements to echo themes of loneliness, emotional overflow, and vulnerability.

For the music video Alone, I drew inspiration from the surrealist language of Rene Magritte, creating an all black, god like look that gives the artist a distant, symbolic, and almost otherworldly presence.

Through styling and makeup, the project constructs a cinematic image of solitude, mystery, and emotional depth.

Escape

For people living in the city, escaping from a place they have stayed in for too long can be a rare chance to relax. After being surrounded by work, bills, and social obligations for so long, there is often a desire to rush out without hesitation.

对于都市人来说，逃离原本待久了的城市是生活中难得放松的机会。被工作、账单、人情事故包裹久了，内心会有一种想要不顾一切冲出去的欲望。这首歌希望能给予各位都市人一丝在精神上放松逃离的支持和帮助。

Sympathy

Everyone experiences moments when life does not go as expected. In Kwanchen's view, human beings are ultimately lonely, and the way to overcome emotional struggle still depends on self regulation.

每个人都会有生活不如意的时候，生命里的某一刻崩溃都能体现出每个人都有其脆弱的一面。只有你内心足够强大，真正地做自己生活的主人，到最后真正“同情”自己的，还是自己。
