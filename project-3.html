Longing & Belonging

We offered tea and opened space for conversation, listening to stories and histories that often go unspoken.

Tea became a quiet thread between the past we never lived and the future we're all somehow going to remember, together.

Everyone in our group comes from an immigrant family, and each of us grew up making tea in different ways.

Through this project, we looked through the lens of our parents: how they carry memories through tea, how they remember, and how they pass it down.
