The Awakening

This project is a campaign video created in response to the fashion brand HubyHu, whose work centres on feminism, sexual liberation, and body liberation.

After interviewing Hu and learning about her SS23 collection, which reimagines marriage as a celebration of women's self love rather than a role defined by being someone's wife, I developed a visual narrative inspired by the Garden of Eden.

The forbidden fruit appears through painted apple makeup, while snakes, as symbols of desire, lust, and rebellion, are translated into body markings, styling, and graphic layout.

Borrowing garments from Hu's previous collections, I mixed and styled them on three female models with different body types to promote a more inclusive image of femininity.

The video presents the "HubyHu girl" as powerful, brave, sensual, and rebellious, using snake shaped typography and a rewritten wedding vow to express female autonomy, desire, and bodily freedom.
